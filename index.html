About Me

Hi, I'm Sherhan. I build web applications, AI powered tools, and automation solutions that solve real problems. Over the past year, I've worked on business websites, ecommerce stores, CRM platforms, and AI driven projects, taking ideas from concept to deployment.

Along the way, I've also worked in SEO and digital marketing, which has helped me understand not just how to build products, but how they reach users and grow after launch. That perspective has influenced the way I approach every project.

Right now, I'm focused on building better software, exploring AI and automation, and continuously improving my skills through hands on projects. I enjoy learning by building, experimenting with new technologies, and turning ideas into products that people can actually use.

What I Offer

Professional Websites

Business and ecommerce websites that load fast, work properly on mobile, and are structured so search engines can actually find them. I care about the details that get skipped: page speed, clean markup, a checkout flow that doesn't lose people.

SEO & Digital Growth

Keyword research, on page optimization, and content that's actually written for the people searching, not just for algorithms. This is the side of my work that taught me how sites perform after launch, and I bring that lens into everything I build.

Modern Web Apps

This is what I enjoy most: building interactive apps and dashboards with React and Next.js, wiring in AI features where they genuinely help, and automating the workflows that would otherwise eat up someone's day.

If you're working on a website, web app, or need help with SEO, feel free to reach out.
